Who are Influencer?

A social media influencer is an individual who utilizes a variety of social media platforms to express their opinions on specific brands or products, consequently influencing their captive audience.


The concept of an influencer is simpleb they re a normal person who is a representative of the general public, honestly reviewing and discussing products and building a relationship of trust with their audience.
